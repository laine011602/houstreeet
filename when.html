WHEN

The majority of the street foods are available throughout the day. However, depending on the time of day and the location, some of these street snacks may be different. The majority of the street foods offered during the day, for example, are snacks or food for in-between meals.

The number and variety of menus, on the other hand, grows towards night. Despite the fact that the majority of the 'daytime' menu is still available, more and more substantial menus are appearing.

Alcohol, particularly 'Sujo' and beer, is the most important and distinguishing feature of Korean street cuisine throughout the evening or night.

The majority of Korean street cuisine is served on moveable carts or temporary structures that can accommodate a limited service area, a restricted cooking area, and plastic chairs.
